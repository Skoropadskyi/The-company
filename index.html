Finovex P2P Lending service

Finovex Ltd was founded in 2016 to facilitate the provision of financial services in the crowdfunding industry through the use of technology and by upholding a higher standard of service and ethics.

Responsibilities:
  • Implementation of Backend REST API
  • Integration with various amazon web services like: SNS, SQS, Lambda, CloudWatch, SES
  • Various refactoring and optimization improving test coverage, updating application architecture
  • Team guidance and mentoring
Implementation of core banking features:
  • Integration with Mambu Global SaaS Banking Platform
  • Custom Golang Mambu Client
  • Precise Type Mappings
  • Support of mambu webhooks and callbacks
Credit risks and reporting features:
  • Built an API for obtaining credit risks, affordability reports and credit history
  • Built Equifax insight share report encoder
  • Support of statement and share reports
  • Automation of the share report exchange between various credit data providers
